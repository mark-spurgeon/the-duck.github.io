Guido[Short Film]

It's the story of a character (very much human, though not physically human) who gets out of the wall he lives in and discovers the real/urban world. He finds positive and negative aspects of the world in which humains live.

Sounds a bit weird? Well, watch the film, and then you'll see that above all, it's an experimental short film that blends different styles of art: 3D, Photography and Graffiti (to some extent).

3D Camera Mapping Videos[Experimental]

Through the years, I've made a few short videos experimenting with camera mapping, a technique consisting of taking an image and mapping it on a 3d object. I can then move the camera around and add 3d objects to the seen.
