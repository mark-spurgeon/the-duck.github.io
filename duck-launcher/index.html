This project is no longer maintained, I am now working on another project.

Duck Launcher is an open source application launcher made for ubuntu, it is a dock similar to Unity's dash and is aimed at providing an interface which lets you access all of the important stuff in just a few clicks. Duck Launcher can also be customized to suit your needs. Speed, productivity and customizibility are at the heart of Duck Launcher's philosophy.
